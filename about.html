What is InfiniX?

InfiniX is a website focused on showcasing games that are currently loved by both casual and professional gamers all around the world.

Our goal is to expand the gaming communities of the games we are showing by giving them exposure. By doing so, more players will have potential interest into playing the games they might not know.

"More players, more fun."
